Scoring Scheme
A member's score in each event is their rank in that event multiplied by the following points:
  • 5 mile run (hh:mm:ss) = 1 point
  • 12 mile ruck (hh:mm:ss) = 2 points
  • 100 meter dash (sec) = 3 points
  • Pull-ups (total in 2 min) = 4 points
  • Push-ups (total in 2 min) = 4 points
  • Sit-ups (total in 2 min) = 4 points
  • Dips (total in 2 min) = 4 points
  • Squat (1RM / bodyweight) = 5 points
  • Bench (1RM / bodyweight) = 5 points
  • Deadlift (1RM / bodyweight) = 5 points
  • Overhead Press (1RM / bodyweight) = 5 points
A member's total score is the sum of the scores in each event.
A lower total score yields a better overall rank (just like Golf).
